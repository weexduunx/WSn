About WAWAW SÉNÉGAL

WAWAW SÉNÉGAL is a pioneering digital entertainment platform, dedicated to bringing the vibrant world of Senegalese and African cinema to viewers around the globe. Our mission is to celebrate African storytelling by providing a diverse range of high-quality films and series that reflect the rich cultural heritage of Africa.

Our Vision

To become the leading streaming platform for African films, showcasing the best of African cinema and empowering African filmmakers to share their stories with the world.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality entertainment that resonates with African audiences, fosters cultural pride, and promotes the diverse voices of African filmmakers.

Our Values

  • Celebrating Diversity
  • Empowering Creators
  • Fostering Community
  • Ensuring Quality
  • Promoting Cultural Heritage
